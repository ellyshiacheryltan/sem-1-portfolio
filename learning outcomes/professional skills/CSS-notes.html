3 Flex-box

  • flex-direction determines the axis and the direction of how the object are organized in the flex-box; either in the direction of a column; organized from top to bottom (column) or bottom to top (column-reverse), or in the direction of a row; organized from left to right (row) or right to left (row-reverse).
  • flex-wrap: wrap is used to move the objects in a flex-box to a new line/row when there’s not enough space to fit all the objects horizontally.
  • justify-content:
    • space-between: creates equal space only between the objects in the flex box, so first and last objects are aligned with the starting and ending line.
    • space-around: objects are distributed evenly in the flex box with equal space around them.
  • align-self: overwrites the alignment of the specific item that’s given this attribute.
